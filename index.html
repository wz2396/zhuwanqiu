LangPal

LangPal is an APP I designed individually for professional langauge learning based on the idea that native speakers can help others who want to learn their language to practice oral speaking. Learners can buy credits with their money and teachers can earn credits by the way of teaching which can be withdrew as money or used to learn other language. In this demo I set the learning language as Chinese and learners can check out the available Chinese native speaker teacher. After looking through their moments, lessons, curriculum they offered and their teaching hours, learners can book an appointment with calendar function or start teir class directly. The app provides three ways to take an lesson - meeting offline, online voice call and online video call - and different way charges different amount of credits. The app also have search function that can help learners quickly find the teachers they need or the lessons they want to learn. Any users can check their home page to see their profile and basic information of teaching hours, learning hours, credit balance and the reviews they sent or received.
